Aarav built a small robot in his room. He named it Nova.
Nova was not just a normal robot. It could learn new things every day. Aarav taught Nova how to clean the table, turn on the lights, and even tell simple jokes.
One day, Aarav felt sad because he failed a test. Nova noticed his face and said, “It is okay. You can try again. I will help you study.”
Nova created a small study plan and explained each topic in simple words. Aarav studied with Nova every evening.
In the next test, Aarav passed with good marks.
Aarav smiled and said, “Thank you, Nova.”
Nova replied, “That is what friends are for.”
